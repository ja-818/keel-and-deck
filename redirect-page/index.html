How it works

More than a chat window

Houston agents don't just answer questions. They do the work, learn from it, and get better every time.

Learns how you work

After every task, your agent gets smarter. It remembers your preferences, your tone, your contacts. Teach it once, it remembers forever.

Works on autopilot

Set recurring tasks. Your agent sends that weekly report every Monday, follows up on pending emails, or checks inventory every morning.

Uses your tools

Connects to Gmail, Google Sheets, QuickBooks, Slack, and 1000+ apps. Your agent works inside the software you already use.

Keeps everything organized

Every task produces files, reports, and documents. All saved in one place. Nothing gets lost in email threads or chat messages.

You stay in control

Review what your agent did before it sends anything. Approve, edit, or redirect. It works for you, not instead of you.

Your data stays private

Runs on your computer. Your files, your emails, your data. Nothing gets sent to servers you don't control.

Want it working while you sleep?

Houston runs on your computer. But if you want your agents working around the clock, we can run them for you. Close your laptop. They keep going.

  • Agents work even when your computer is off
  • Everything stays the same, nothing to set up again
  • Check in from your phone or browser, anytime
  • Recurring tasks run on schedule, no reminders needed
