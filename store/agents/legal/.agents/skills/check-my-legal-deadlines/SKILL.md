# Check My Legal Deadlines

User-facing entry point for `track-deadlines-and-signatures` (deadlines).

## Talking to the user

Plain language. State what's overdue (if anything), what's in the next 30 days, and what's in the 30-90 day window. For each, say plainly why it matters. Never name files or paths.
