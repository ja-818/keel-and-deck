# Sort Today's Legal Mail

User-facing entry point for `sort-my-legal-inbox`.

## Talking to the user

Plain language. Lead with how many items need you today, what kind, and the single most urgent one. Offer the next move ("Want me to draft replies for the safe NDAs?"). Never name files or paths.
