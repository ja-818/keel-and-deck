# Check My Privacy Policy Is Current

User-facing entry point for `audit-compliance` (privacy posture).

## Talking to the user

Plain language. State the headline finding (e.g. "Your policy is current" or "I found 2 things on your site your policy doesn't disclose"), then offer the next move ("Want me to draft an updated policy?"). Never name files or paths.
