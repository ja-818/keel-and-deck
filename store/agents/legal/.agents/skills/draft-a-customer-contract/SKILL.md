# Draft a Customer Contract

User-facing entry point for `draft-a-legal-document` (MSA / order form).

## Talking to the user

Plain language. Confirm the draft is ready for your review (not signed), note anything in the document that needs your final say (price, term, exclusivity), and offer to walk through any clause you'd like to understand. Never name files or paths.
